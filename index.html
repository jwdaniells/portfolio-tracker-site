Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need to be technical to use these?

+

Not at all. Setup is guided — Claude asks you questions and builds your tracker from your answers. No coding, no formulas, no configuration files needed.

Can I buy modules separately and add more later?

+

Yes. Each module is sold separately and works independently. If you start with the Portfolio Tracker and later want the Retirement Planner, you can purchase it separately. The bundle is simply the best-value option if you want all three.

What Claude subscription do I need?

+

You'll need a Claude Pro subscription or above (from £18/month at claude.ai). The modules live inside Claude Projects, which is a Pro feature. This is separate from your module purchase.

Is my financial data safe and private?

+

Yes. Your modules live entirely within your own Claude account. We never see your data — no third-party servers, no data synchronisation, no open-banking connections. Everything stays in your Claude Project and you own it completely.

What UK account types are supported?

+

SIPP, Stocks & Shares ISA, Lifetime ISA, General Investment Account, Workplace Pension (defined contribution and defined benefit), and cash holdings. The Retirement Planner specifically supports DB pension income streams and State Pension start dates.

Is this financial advice?

+

No. These are modelling and tracking tools. They help you organise, visualise and project your own financial data — they do not make investment recommendations or constitute regulated financial advice. For personalised advice, please consult a qualified financial adviser.
